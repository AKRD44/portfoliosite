The sounds of imagination

Sounds of imagination is a website built in React.js and hosted on an AWS S3 bucket. Simply write a few lines then click on the phonograph to hear your words come to life. Through AWS Lambda, I tokenize the input words, vectorize them with word2vec and try to come up with the best 2 sound effects that match the given text. So far the page will always provide 2 audio clips even if I don't have a proper sound effect for what's requested (so you might sometimes gets some weird sound effects)
Check it out at The sounds of imagination

MAP FROM THE PAST

Built with Django REST API in the back end with React/Redux in the front end. The site allows users to upload pictures of what places used to look like before. They can place that picture at the right location on a map alongside and show what the place looks like now by adjusting the google maps streetview.
Check it out at Map from the past
